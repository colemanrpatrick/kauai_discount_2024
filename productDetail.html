Backed by sea cliffs and overlooking the ocean, Aloha Kai Luau features a beautiful setting unlike any other luau on Oahu. Held right next to Sea Life Park Hawaii, Aloha Kai Luau invites you to experience a traditional Hawaiian luau- a memorable evening filled with delicious foods and island-style entertainment.

Upon arrival, you'll be warmly welcomed with a Hawaiian lei and can then watch or participate in a variety of cultural presentations, including a hula or ukulele lesson, flower lei making, coconut headband weaving and Polynesian tattoos. There will also be several mini-performances, including coconut tree climbing and a coconut demonstration.

Then sit down to a mouthwatering Hawaiian luau dinner. On the menu are a wide variety of delicious foods (see menu below). Dinner is followed by a spectacular Polynesian show featuring an award-winning cast, highlighting the vibrant traditions of Hawaii and the love of the sea as you are taken on a voyage across Polynesia. The show culminates with a sword of fire!
