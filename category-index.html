Kauai Activities

Kauai is the perfect tropical playground for a large variety of activities. Many activities take place in or around the water. Enjoy a snorkeling tour along the remote Na Pali Coast or over to the island of Niihau, go on a big game fishing tour, go scuba diving, kayaking, sailing or surfing.

If you have never been to a traditional Hawaiian luau, attend one on Kauai. A luau is a traditional Hawaiian feast with many delicious foods, coupled with cultural demonstrations and a Polynesian show featuring music and dances from Hawaii and greater Polynesia. Attending at least one luau is a must for every visitor to Hawaii.
